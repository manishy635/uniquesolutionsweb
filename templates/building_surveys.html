Building surveying has been a profession from 1970s, and covers a large gamut of subjects, namely, construction design and building works. Besides that it also includes project management and monitoring, property legislation advice. Insurance assessment and claims assistance together with defect investigation and maintenance advice is also an important aspect of building surveys. Building surveyss to ensure compliance with building regulations is often resorted to by the Government agencies tasked with ensuring compliance of approved building plans. Quite often a dispute arises on the actual measurement of building in order to settle disputed payment of bills or even to assess the actual condition of the building. All the above come under building surveys.
