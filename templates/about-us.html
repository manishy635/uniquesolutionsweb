ABOUT GEO SOLUTIONS

Unique Solutions is an organization, which is committed to promote Geo-informatics and its applications in various development and planning activities. Our main focus is on Rural Development and Decentralized Planning. Geo Solutions is established by a team of Geographers, Social Workers, Regional & Urban Planners, Geo Informatics Professionals and Information Solution Specialists to serve the community through Geographical Sciences.
